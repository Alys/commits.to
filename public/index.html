The I-Will System a.k.a. "promises.to" a.k.a. "commits.to"

This is a commitment logger. Create a commitment by constructing a URL like "alice.promises.to/send_the_report" and just by clicking on such a URL, a promise is logged here. We're working on the rest of the implementation, where you can mark promises fulfilled and everyone can see statistics on your reliability.
